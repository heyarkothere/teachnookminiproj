Historical Places of INDIA

Red Fort

It is a historic fort in Old Delhi,India that served as the main residence of the Mughal Emperors.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal was commissioned by Shah Jahan in 1631, to be built in the memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Qutub Minar

The Qutb Minar was built over the ruins of the Lal Kot, the citadel of Dhillika.

Fatehpur Sikiri

Fatehpur Sikri was founded as the capital of Mughal Empire in 1571 by Emperor Akbar, serving this role from 1571 to 1585

India Gate

India Gate came into existence in December 1918 under the British rule for building war graves and memorials to soldiers who were killed in the First World War.

Victoria Memorial

The Victoria Memorial is a large marble building on the Maidan in Central Kolkata,in the memory of Queen Victoria.
